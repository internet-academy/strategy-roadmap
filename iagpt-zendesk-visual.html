What's happening today

Zendesk currently holds 58 approved support knowledge articles and operational workflows. Our 3–4 support staff must search 3 separate systems (Zendesk, Confluence, internal docs) to find answers — wasting an estimated 2 hours per person per week.

The 4 key problems

1. Vendor dependency

IA does not control the knowledge platform. If Zendesk raises prices or changes features, we have no leverage.

2. Recurring cost (~¥4–4.8M/year)

Suite Team, 4 agents at $55/agent/month. This cost continues every year regardless of how much we actually use Zendesk.

3. Knowledge fragmentation

Support knowledge is split across 3 systems. Staff waste ~2h/week searching — that's ~¥8.3M/year in lost staff time (4 staff × 2h × 52 weeks × ¥20K/h).

4. No AI integration path

Zendesk knowledge can't be used in IAGPT AI workflows. Staff can't get AI-assisted answers grounded in support knowledge.

2 — THE IDEAL SITUATION: How Phase 2 Solves These Problems

Target: one unified knowledge base, AI-powered, IA-controlled

Bring approved Zendesk knowledge into IAGPT. Staff search one system instead of three. Eventually replace Zendesk entirely when we're ready.

How each problem gets solved

Vendor dependency → IA-controlled

Knowledge moves into IAGPT, which IA owns and controls. No more vendor lock-in.

Recurring cost → Eliminated (after Phase 4)

Once Zendesk is cancelled (target: Feb 2027), ~¥4–4.8M/year stops flowing out. Phase 2 builds the foundation for this decision.

Fragmentation → One unified search

Staff search IAGPT only — Zendesk + Confluence knowledge in one place. ~2h/week per person reclaimed immediately (from July 2026).

No AI path → AI-assisted answers

IAGPT provides AI-assisted answers grounded in support knowledge. Staff get better answers faster, with human review for quality.

Phase 2 approach: two tracks

Phase 2A — Knowledge Ingestion

  • 58 approved Zendesk articles imported into staff-only IAGPT
  • Support / School Desk reviews each article for accuracy
  • Staff search and use IAGPT for support answers
  • Estimated effort: ~87h (review + ingest + test)

Phase 2B — Replacement Readiness

  • Identify chat, ticketing, escalation, reporting workflows
  • Categorize each: "replace with IAGPT" or "retain in Zendesk"
  • Gap analysis — what must be built before cancellation
  • No replacement work in Phase 2 — only inventory and planning
